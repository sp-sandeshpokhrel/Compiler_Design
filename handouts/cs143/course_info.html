Grading

  • 4 Written Assignments: 10% (2.5% each)
  • 4 Programming Assignments: 50%
    • I, II: 10% each
    • III, IV: 15% each
  • Midterm I: 20%
  • Midterm II: 20%
We will be using Gradescope for written assignment submission.

Textbook

There is no required textbook. You can optionally use “Compilers: Principles, Techniques, and Tools” by Aho, Lam, Sethi and Ullman as a reference.

Handouts

All lectures notes, handouts and assignments will be uploaded on the class website.

Exams

There are two exams for this class, a midterm and final. The midterm will be cumulative (so all material that was covered up to the midterm), but the final will NOT be cumulative; it will only cover material from the midterm onwards.
